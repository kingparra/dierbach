 Figure 10-7, Arithmetic Operator Special Methods
**************************************************

+--------------------------+---------------------------+---------------------+
|  Operator                |  Example Use              |  Special Method     |
+==========================+===========================+=====================+
|  ``-`` (negation)        |  ``-frac1``               |  ``__neg__``        |
+--------------------------+---------------------------+---------------------+
|  ``+`` (addition)        |  ``frac1 + frac2``        |  ``__add__``        |
+--------------------------+---------------------------+---------------------+
|  ``-`` (subtraction)     |  ``frac1 - frac2``        |  ``__sub__``        |
+--------------------------+---------------------------+---------------------+
|  ``*`` (multiplication)  |  ``frac1 * frac2``        |  ``__mul__``        |
+--------------------------+---------------------------+---------------------+
