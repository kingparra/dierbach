********************************************************************
 Figure 2-19: Operator Precedence of Arithmetic Operators in Python
********************************************************************

+-----------------------------------------+--------------------------+
|  Operator                               |           Associativity  |
+=========================================+==========================+
|  ``**`` (exponentiation)                |           right-to-left  |
+-----------------------------------------+--------------------------+
|  ``-`` (negation)                       |           left-to-right  |
+-----------------------------------------+--------------------------+
|  ``*``, ``/``, ``//``, ``%``            |           left-to-right  |
+-----------------------------------------+--------------------------+
|  ``+``, ``-``                           |           left-to-right  |
+-----------------------------------------+--------------------------+
